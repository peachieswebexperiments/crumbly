01

Steamed Lemon-Lime Marmalade Sponge Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 4 tbsp Marmalade

Step Method

Prep: Cream butter and sugar until pale. Beat in eggs one by one and add vanilla. Fold in dry ingredients gently.

Assembly: Grease basin, add marmalade to the bottom, and top with batter.

Seal: Cover tightly with parchment and foil. Secure with twine if needed.

Steam: Steam for 50-60 minutes in a water bath halfway up the bowl.

Serve: Rest for 5 mins, then invert onto a plate to let the marmalade glaze cascade down.

02

Blueberry Lemon Skillet Scones

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 cup Butter (cold)
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup Blueberry jam

The Dough

Whisk dry ingredients. Rub in cold butter until it reaches a coarse crumb texture. Combine egg, lemon, and vanilla separately, then merge into dry ingredients to form a stiff dough.

Fold in the blueberry jam gently—you want beautiful purple streaks, not a solid color.

Skillet Baking

Shape into a disk and cut into 8 wedges. Heat a greased skillet over low heat. Cook covered for 8–10 minutes per side.

Pro Tip: Keep the heat very low so the centers bake through without scorching the golden exterior.

03

Zesty Lemon Skillet Crepes

The Essentials

Batter Base

1 cup Flour, 2 Eggs, 2 tbsp Sugar, 2 tbsp Melted Butter, 1 tsp Vanilla

Citrus Kick

4 tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice

The Filling

1/2 cup French Wild Blueberry Jam

1. Whisk

Combine eggs, sugar, butter, vanilla, and juice. Vigorously whisk in flour to avoid any lumps. Adjust with juice if too thick.

2. Swirl

Pour thin batter into a buttered medium-heat skillet. Tilt to coat. Cook until edges are golden and top sets.

3. Fill & Fold

Spread blueberry jam over the warm crepe. Fold into quarters or roll for a beautiful presentation.

04

No-Bake Blueberry & Marmalade Parfait

LAYER

Components

Base Toasted Flour & Butter Crumbles
Berry Blueberry Jam with Lemon Juice
Zest Marmalade Zesty Sauce
Chill 30 mins Refrigeration

The Golden Crumb

In a skillet, melt butter, sugar, and vanilla. Stir in flour and soda. "Toast" for 5-8 minutes until golden and nutty. This creates a deeply flavored, cooked cookie-like base that provides the essential crunch.

The Assembly

Thin the jam and marmalade with lemon juice. In tall glasses, layer: 2 tbsp crumbles, blueberry mixture, more crumbles, then marmalade. Repeat. Chill to allow flavors to meld and textures to soften slightly.
