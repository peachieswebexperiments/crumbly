CRUMBLY Signature Series

The Blueberry Lemon Marmalade Loaf

Precision-engineered for that perfect "cakey" rise.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour 174g
  • Granulated Sugar 70g
  • Unsalted Butter 60g
  • Vegetable Oil 140g
  • Plain Yogurt 260g
  • Large Eggs (2) ~110g
  • Lemon Marmalade 40g
  • Blueberry Jam 100g
  • Leavening
    Baking Powder 6g
    Baking Soda 2g
    Fine Salt 2g

! The Full Method

1. The Sandy Base

Combine 174g flour, 70g sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the 60g butter and 140g oil. Mix until it resembles moist sand. This "Reverse Mixing" coats the flour in fat, preventing gluten development for a crumbly, cakey texture.

2. The Liquid Bind

In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, 260g yogurt, and 40g lemon marmalade until emulsified. Slowly pour this into your sandy base while mixing on low speed until just smooth.

3. The Signature Swirl

Transfer batter to a lined loaf pan. Dollop 100g of wild blueberry jam across the top and swirl with a knife in a figure-8 pattern.

4. The Bake & Cool

Bake at 175°C for **45–55 minutes**. When done, turn the oven off and crack the door for **5–10 minutes** before removing. This "Slow Cool" prevents the center from sinking.

Pro Tip: For a less sweet result, ensure you use plain, unsweetened yogurt.
