Velvet Blueberry &
Golden Marmalade

An curated anthology of seasonal delights, blending the tart sweetness of sun-ripened berries with the zesty warmth of golden citrus.

1. Blueberry Velvet Thumbprints

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Blueberry jam

Instructions

01. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

02. In a large bowl, cream together 1 cup of softened butter and 1/2 cup of sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.

03. Slowly incorporate 2 cups of flour into the butter mixture until a soft, cohesive dough forms.

04. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough and roll them into smooth balls.

05. Place the balls on a baking sheet, spaced 2 inches apart.

06. Using your thumb or the back of a small spoon, press a deep indentation into the center of each ball.

07. Fill each cavity with approximately half a teaspoon of blueberry jam.

08. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the edges are just beginning to turn a pale golden brown.

09. Allow the cookies to cool completely on the baking sheet so the jam sets into a velvet-like texture.

2. Golden Marmalade Shortbread Bars

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Marmalade
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

01. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a square baking pan.

02. In a medium bowl, combine 1.5 cups of flour and 1/4 cup of sugar.

03. Cut in 3/4 cup of chilled butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

04. Firmly press two-thirds of this crumble mixture into the bottom of the pan.

05. Stir together 1/2 cup of marmalade and 1 tablespoon of lemon juice.

06. Spread the marmalade mixture evenly over the shortbread base.

07. Sprinkle the remaining flour-butter crumbs over the fruit layer.

08. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until the topping is golden and bubbling.

09. Let the bars cool entirely before slicing into squares.

3. Midnight-Sun Swirl Tarts

Ingredients

  • Flour & Butter
  • Sugar
  • Blueberry jam
  • Marmalade
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

01. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

02. Mix 1 cup flour, 2 tbsp sugar, and 1/2 cup cold butter into a stiff dough.

03. Press dough into muffin tin cups to create thin tart shells.

04. Mix 1/4 cup blueberry jam with a teaspoon of lemon juice.

05. Mix 1/4 cup marmalade with a teaspoon of lemon juice.

06. Place one tsp of blueberry mixture into one side of each shell.

07. Place one tsp of marmalade mixture into the other side.

08. Use a toothpick to gently swirl the two jams together.

09. Bake for 15 minutes until pastry is crisp. Cool before removing.

4. Lemon-Glazed Blueberry Biscuits

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Blueberry jam
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

01. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

02. Whisk together 2 cups of flour and 2 tablespoons of sugar.

03. Grate 1/2 cup of frozen butter into flour and toss lightly.

04. Add 1/3 cup of blueberry jam; fold gently until streaks appear.

05. Pat dough into a disc approximately 1 inch thick on a floured surface.

06. Cut into circles and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

07. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes until risen and golden.

08. Whisk 3 tbsp sugar and 1 tbsp lemon juice for the glaze.

09. Drizzle over warm biscuits and serve immediately.
